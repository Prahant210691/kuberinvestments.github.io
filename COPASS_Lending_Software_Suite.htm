4. Ezyb - Loan Management System (LMS)

Overview: The LMS manages all approved loans from Stream LOS and COPS, handling post-sanction processes such as disbursement, accounting, collection tracking, and reporting. It is the financial engine of the lending ecosystem Unified loan servicing engine for all loan types.

 

💰 Loan Account Management

         Unique loan account creation

         Support for linked co-borrowers and guarantors

         Loan tagging with source (partner, agent, field officer) for reporting

         Amortization schedule generation

         GL code mapping for transactions

📈 Accounting & Transactions

         Multiple repayment frequency support (monthly, weekly, fortnightly, daily)

         Auto-application of payments to components (penalty → interest → principal)

         Auto-recalculation of balance after early payment or partial pre-closure

         Integration with GL master for correct debit/credit postings

         Charge master for flexible fee structures (processing, documentation, etc.)

         Reversals and penalty tracking

🔄 Repayment Handling & Collections

         Demand generation based on amortization or flat schedules

         Manual, mandate-based, or QR-based collection modes

         Real-time syncing with field apps (e.g., AppCo) for repayment updates

         Bounce management and fee posting (auto and manual)

         Partial payment support and advance installment tracking

         Overdue bucket creation for DPD analysis

📉 Loan Status Tracking

         Real-time status (active, overdue, closed)

         Delinquency aging (DPD 190+), NPA flagging as per product rules

         Closed loan archiving with search and filter options

⚖️ Compliance & Reporting

         Audit trail for every transaction and data change

         Maker-checker workflows for adjustments

         User-level activity logs for access control and approvals

         P&L, balance sheet, and trial balance

         Branch-wise and product-wise performance dashboards

         Daily collection, due, and demand reports

         Aging analysis and portfolio at risk (PAR) reports

         Income/charge recovery tracking

         Forecasting module for upcoming due amounts

         Downloadable reports in Excel/PDF formats

🏢 Organizational Setup & Flexibility

         Role-based access control (admin, ops, auditor, collection, etc.)

         Configurable loan products (interest, fees, tenure, grace period, etc.)

         Master data for GL, charges, documents, sources, and users

✅ Integrations

         CBS/EzyB: Loan account creation, disbursement push, repayment pull

         AppCo: Field collection sync (offline and real-time)

         OneGlance: For dashboards and analytics integration

         SMS/Email APIs: Customer notifications and reminders

         Leegality/Digio: E-sign and e-mandate integration

         Credit Bureau: Periodic loan performance sharing
